Introduction to El Filibusterismo

El Filibusterismo was written from 1888 to 1891, at Brussels, Madrid. It was published at 1891. The Genre of this novel is Novel and Colonial Literature. El Filibusterismo addresses the issues about the abusive clergy, racism to Filipino people, and the need to have a big change in politics which is the political reform.

Why It Matters Today?

Rizal's El Filibusterismo gave a spark on everyone's minds when it comes to reality. The Philippines, having 110 million people, others still suffers from poverty and injustice that Rizal included in his novels, Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo. These novels teaches us about the maltreatment of government and inspires Filipinos to fight for their rights.

José Rizal's Biography

Birth Date: June 19 1861, Calamba, Laguna

Died: December 30 1896, Manila

Roles: He is a Writer, Reformist, Doctor (Physician), and Nationalist.

Major Contribution: Rizal contributed novels to advocate about the political and social issues in the Philippines. His novels Noli Me Tangere "Touch Me Not" and El Filibusterismo "The Reign of Greed" shows the discriminatory, biased, and injustices of Spanish Government.
